Game rules

You can pick 6 numbers out from 1 to 49. Later, the system will randomly pick out 6 normal numbers and 1 special number. The money you win depends on how many numbers you and the randomly picked numbers have in common.

3 same numbers

You can get $400 (3 normal numbers or 2 normal and 1 special).

4 same numbers

You win $2000 for 4 normal numbers and $1000 for 3 normal numbers and 1 special number.

5 same numbers

You win $100000 for 5 normal numbers and $50000 for 4 normal numbers and 1 special number.

6 same numbers

You win $200000000 for 6 normal numbers and $100000000 for 5 normal numbers and 1 special number.
